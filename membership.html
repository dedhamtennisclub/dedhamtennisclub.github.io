New Member Applications

  1. New members must be Dedham residents. (Members moving from Dedham may retain their membership)
  2. Children of existing members must apply for their own adult membership at age 26.
  3. Prevailing initiation fee will be paid by all new members except:
    • Children applying, as stated above.
    • Either divorced member, both wishing to retain membership.
    • Prior member returning to Dedham residency. (Also eligible to be considered for immediate reinstatement upon receipt of written request by the President or Admissions and Membership executive.)
  4. The Executive Committee will consider new member applications after all of the following are received by the Administration and Membership committee:
    • Completed Application Form
    • Letter of endorsement from proposer
    • Letters of endorsement from two seconders
  5. Mail completed application to:
