Dedham Tennis Club Ground Rules - 2022

  • Please do not play on courts that have not sufficiently dried following heavy rains
    • If you leave footprints in the clay, it is too wet to play!
  • All members and guests are required to wear suitable tennis attire, not necessarily white
    • Shirts must be worn at all times
  • Smooth soled tennis shoes must be worn
    • No running or cleated shoes, “docksiders”, or bare feet
  • All play areas, including the pickelball and backboard area, are to be used for play only
  • All members and guests are expected to follow proper tennis etiquette and to be mindful of the Club’s neighbors
  • Please sweep the courts and lines after you have finished play
    • Nets should be left up and not lowered at the end of the day
  • No gas, charcoal, or other cooking is allowed except for Club sanctioned events
  • The Club closes at sundown

Dedham Tennis Club Guest Policy - 2022

  • No guests may play without a member present
  • All courts are available for member-guest play, except during Free Guest Hours
  • Guest fees are $10 per guest, per court reservation, except during Free Guest Hours
    • Guest names must be entered into YourCourts in order to complete the reservation
  • The guest policy and reservation system includes the pickleball court
  • Each membership is allowed a maximum of 10 billable guest spots per season
  • A guest is allowed to play no more than 2 times per month at the Club, regardless of being invited by different members
  • Members are allowed 1 guest during primetime hours and up to 3 guests during non-primetime hours 
    • Primetime hours are all day Saturday, Sunday & Holidays and weekdays 4:30pm – 8:00pm
  • Courts may be reserved for up to 120 minutes and guests may play for the entire reservation period
  • Each membership is granted 6 free passes for immediate non-member family to play as guests
    • These passes will not be counted against your 10 guests per season allotment
    • Immediate family (not part of your family membership) is defined as spouse, child, sibling, parent
  • Free Guest Hours
    • Monday - Friday from 8:00-10:00am, each member may bring up to 3 guests, one day a week, for play on courts 2, 3 & 4 and the Pickleball court
    • Play during this time will not be subject to guest fees or count against a member's ten guest limit
    • Members will be required to use YourCourts to reserve courts and list all player's names
    • The same guest may not play more than 2 times a month with any member
    • The YourCourts system will be monitored to ensure information is complete
