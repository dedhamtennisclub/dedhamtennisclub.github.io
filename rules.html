Ground Rules

  1. Please sign yourself in before you play, in addition to any guests. Guest fees are based on number of visits.
    • $50 for members bringing guests to play fewer than 10 times
    • $100 for members bringing guests 10 or more times per season
  2. Children under 19 have priority on courts 3 and 4, Monday – Friday from 1 PM to 4 PM, except holidays. At all other times, member’s children must give way to waiting adults (unless playing with at least one adult).
  3. Nets should be left up and not be lowered at the end of the day.
  4. Smooth soled tennis sneakers must be worn. No running shoes, cleated shoes, “docksiders”, or bare feet. If you leave footprints in the clay, it is too wet to play.
  5. All members and guests are required to wear suitable tennis attire (not necessarily white). Shirts must be worn at all times.
  6. If other adult members are waiting, no non-tournament match shall continue past (whichever is shorter):
    • Two completed sets, or
    • 1.5 hours for doubles or 1 hour for singles
  7. Please sweep the courts and lines after you have finished play!
  8. Please do not play on courts that have not sufficiently dried following heavy rains.

Follow the Tennis Lesson Policy adopted 10/22/17
